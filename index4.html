Conclusion


Our project is about finding the relationship between personal life and academic performance within 50 students of Batch 2027 PSHS SMC. The results present valuable insights into the academic achievements in Batch 2027, emphasizing the prevalence of positive parent-student relationships, relationship statuses, and remarkable academic performance. While the findings offer a foundation for understanding this relationship, this can be further investigated. These insights could contribute to the development of effective strategies to support students, especially those with lower academic performance, in their academic journeys.
